---

## Structure

The essay is structured into three main sections:

1. **Case for achieving the target**
   - Momentum in solar capacity additions
   - Declining battery and solar PV costs
   - Projected generation based on capacity-growth trends

2. **Case against achieving the target**
   - Political instability and budget constraints
   - Rise of climate-sceptic parties
   - Cannibalisation effect and declining capture rates
   - Cuts to renewable subsidies

3. **Discussion and conclusion**
   - Evaluates both cases
   - Argues that without increased public investment, the target is unlikely to be met
   - Highlights battery deployment as a key but insufficient stabilising factor
